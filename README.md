🗺️ What if a CV could tell a story — on a map?

I'm excited to share my Interactive Geospatial CV, built to reflect who I am as a geospatial professional: someone who thinks spatially, works with maps, and believes data should be visual.

The CV features:
📍 Interactive map with location pins across Indonesia
📅 Chronological timeline of work, education & research
🎯 Filter by category (Work / Education / Research)
📊 Skill visualization with animated bars

From my early days studying Cartography & Remote Sensing at UGM, to working as a Researcher Assistant at LAPAN, an Agrarian Reform Consultant at BPN, and now as a Lecturer at Sinar Mas Berau Coal Polytechnic — every pin on that map represents a chapter of my professional journey.
